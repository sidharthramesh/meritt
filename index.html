Social Banking on the Blockchain

Inclusive lending at fair rates

What is it?

The Meritt Finance Protocol allows for Financial Instruments on the blockchain for decentralised financial services using Bitcoin, Ethereum, ERC20 tokens

How does it work?

Social lending is peer-to-peer loans within your network.
Marketplace lending makes it available to everyone

Social lending platform for friends, trusted communities and the unbanked

Friends, family or communities can create or join their trusted network on Meritt and directly provide P2P loans to members on their terms under the group’s governance. Users can access P2P loans from their Meritt trusted networks.

For example, rotating savings and credit association called “partner system” in Jamaica can now be easily transposed online by anyone on Meritt with a connected mobile phone.



Marketplace and framework for global P2P lendin

P2P loans at fair rates on the marketplace for participants and decentralised organisations.

Flexible requirements: build your Meritt score or provide a token collateral to access the marketplace.

Lenders choose their risk/return category and invest in marketplace P2P loans directly or use our algorithmic investment solutions for automated diversification

Disclaimer

Loans are not made by MERITT Lending. MERITT Lending is a peer to peer decentralised marketplace. Only participants are responsible for review and approval. For example, a one year €10,000 loan with a rate of 10.00% APR would have 12 scheduled monthly payments of €880. Annual percentage rates (APRs) through MERITT vary. Loan requests are only fulfilled if a sufficient pool of liquidity is available. Your investments may lose value; no MERITT or bank guarantee is provided.
